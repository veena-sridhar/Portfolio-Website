About

After finishing my degree Chemistry at UC San Diego, I started working at a biotech company in San Diego. My job there required quite a bit of familiarity with some laboratory data management websites and software. Pretty soon, this sparked my interest in web development, and I took an online class from Thinkful to learn more. With this, I was hooked! If I wasn't coding, I was always thinking about it no matter what else I was doing. I still get so consumed by it that I'd love to turn it into a career! Currently, I am looking for contract opportunities in front-end development. Check out my blog to find out more about my learning journey!
